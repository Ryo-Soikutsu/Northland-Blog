# 2025

## Backstory

You're a new data analyst intern who has recently been recruited by the International Contract Agency (ICA). As part of your first assignment, your supervisor has attached you to an ongoing contract.

The primary target is William Knox, the CISO of Kronstadt Industries. Kronstadt Industries is secretly a world-leading supplier for next-gen military technologies, and has recently come under fire for selling weapons to a third-world dictator, which led to 34 innocent protestors killed. The shadow client is interested to see what new projects and developments the company has kept under wraps, and has hired the ICA to find out.

William Knox is an incredibly paranoid individual, and keeps no social media or online presence. He never shares his location outside of his trusted circle, and he has made the lives of our data analysts hell trying to track him down. Recently, his secretary has hired a new intern, who has a much weaker sense of OPSEC than William himself. Perhaps you can take advantage of this weakness and strike while there is a chance
